About Us

Welcome to Opportunify, the platform that empowers university students to succeed in the finance industry. We offer a tracker with thousands of opportunities worldwide and a mentoring platform to help students navigate the complex process of applying for finance internships and jobs. Our focus is on providing equal opportunities for all, regardless of background or identity.

At Opportunify, inclusivity and equal opportunities are our core values. We believe that everyone should have an equal chance to succeed, and we're committed to helping students break into the finance industry, regardless of their gender, race, socio-economic status or academic background. Our name, Opportunify, reflects our mission to open up opportunities for everyone and make them accessible. Join us at Opportunify and take the first step towards a successful career in finance!
